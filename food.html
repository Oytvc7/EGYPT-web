My favorite food in Egypt

Kushari

Koshari is one of the most popular Egyptian dishes. It consists of pasta, rice, black lentils, fried onions, and tomato sauce, which is hot and fried tomatoes. Koshari is considered a low-cost, high-calorie dish with oxidized vegetable oils due to frying, which raises a lot of health criticism about the meal and the like

Ful w Taameya

Ta'ameya (Egyptian falafel) is an all-time favorite street food in Egypt. In most parts of the Middle East, falafel is made with ground chickpeas. In Egypt however, we make it with dried fava beans. These are best served with pita bread, tomato, onions, and tahini sauce
